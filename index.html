About the Product

What is Tap & Bap?

Tap & Bap is a revolutionary smartwatch engineered for people who rely on epinephrine auto-injectors (EpiPens) to manage life-threatening allergic reactions. In a crisis, fumbling for a pen in a bag or pocket can cost precious seconds — seconds that can determine survival.

Worn directly on the wrist, Tap & Bap integrates a compact, precision-loaded needle mechanism that can deploy the EpiPen dose with a single firm tap on the watch face. The device is activated through a deliberate two-step press to prevent accidental discharge, yet responds within milliseconds when needed. It is refillable, approved for standard epinephrine cartridges, and pairs with a companion app to track expiry dates, injection history, and emergency contact alerts.

Designed with input from allergists, paramedics, and patients, Tap & Bap empowers users — including children, elderly individuals, and those who may be alone during a reaction — to administer potentially life-saving medication faster than any traditional auto-injector. It's not just a watch. It's a second chance.
