What We Do
THREE FEATURES.
ONE LIFE-SAVING APP.

Tap & Bap was built for people with allergies who need real protection — not generic wellness features. Every tool in this app is purpose-built for critical moments.

🚨

911 in a Tap

One-touch emergency calling when you're suffering an allergic reaction. No fumbling, no menus. The moment you can't speak, your phone speaks for you — location, allergy profile, and all.

⭐

Restaurant Reviews

Browse real reviews from allergy-aware diners. See exactly which restaurants have had allergy incidents, how they responded, and what dishes are safest for your specific allergens.

📷

Camera Ingredient Scan

Point your iPhone camera at any food label, menu, or dish. Tap & Bap cross-references your saved allergen profile and instantly alerts you if something you're about to eat is dangerous.

Simple & Fast
HOW IT
WORKS
👤

Set Up Your Profile

Input your allergies once. Tap & Bap remembers them across every feature — from scans to emergency alerts.

📍

Search & Dine Smart

Browse allergy-reviewed restaurants near you. Read incident reports, check safety scores, and dine with confidence.

📷

Scan Before You Eat

Use the live camera scanner to check any food for your allergens. Instant red/green alerts right on screen.

🚨

Emergency — One Tap

Reaction starting? Hit the big red button. 911 is called immediately with your location and allergy profile shared automatically.
